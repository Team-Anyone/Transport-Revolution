PROJECT-OUTLINE

Outlook

(FIGURES USED IN EXPLANATION CAN VARY, THEY ARE TAKEN JUST FOR EASY UNDERSTANDING, FIGURES ARE DERIVED THROUGH NEWTON'S LAWS OF MOTION) The middle section of the main road is divided into two lanes with proper barrigation.One lane is for the forward and the other app is for the backward movement.On these lanes there are pods(autonomous or self-driven) running continuously with 60kmph.There are elevated tracks for the loading and unloading of the passengers on the definite station of which the passenger has taken tickets. The pods stop only at the desired station of the two passengers sitting on the pods, hence reducing the waiting time.For instance, the maximum speed of delhi metro is 90kmph but the average time reduces to 27kmph due to various stoppages.This system charges just charges 1 rupee per kilometre to each passenger, in case a passenger wants to board the pod singularly, he/she can pay 2 rupees.This pod is cheaper than all the current means of transports while providing all the high quality facilities like air bags, air-conditioning, first aid box, gps tracking etc (mentioned in detail in the ppt). The pods are centrally monitored and controlled. In case of a faulty pod, all the pods running on the same track are made to stop and a helper pod present at each station toes it to the next station for damage repair. > )

A SENSOR is placed at a distance of 282.84 metres from the end where the compartment joins the main track. If the SENSOR is found to be without pressure at (no of blocks joined to travel together +2 for space and safety) continuous unit of time required by the block to travel distance equal to its length; a signal is sent and the first set of ( joined) compartments is prepared to join the track at the opening. When any person reaches the destination point, the compartment detaches from the main track and moves to an auxiliary track in the same way as during joining and takes the compartment to the booth with decreasing acceleration and ultimately stoping it at desired booth. The complete setup reduces cases of theft of goods and bullying or physical harrassments caused in public transports due to its locking feature. These VEHICLES BEING SELF DRIVEN CAN LOOK FOR THE TRACK CONDITION THEMSELVES THROUGH COMPUTER VISION and IR sensors. IN case of families A special carriage cabin is made which can be joined to the main pod in case their are children travelling with families. Also, pods equipped with all the ambulance facilities are made for the emergency cases, as all the pods are centrally controlled, the ambulance pods can be made to reach the hospital very quickly.

ISSUE

EVERINCREASING population and automobiles AND exponentially increasing TRAFFIC and ENVIRONMENT-THREATS.

OUR IDEA AND ITS BENEFITS

To build Fast and efficient transport system which uses small SELF DRIVEN oval shaped compartments to carry people safely with high speed on tracks laid on roads which will help reducing pollution and ensure faster travelling.

SAVE FUEL

Reduced waiting hours ensures sustainable usage of fuels. THE STRUCTURE will also remove the illegal accession of land by road-side vendors.The space optimisation allows more peple to travel through a given space( the method reduces the cases of one person taking an entire four seater car for travel)

REDUCE NOISE AND AIR POLLUTION

Honking in hilly areas causes landslides and disturbs wildlife and in plains causes several temperamental problems .Whereas soot from vehicles causes air pollution and lung diseases. ( These compartments dont honk and are electrically driven)

SECURED TRANSFER AND TRACKING OF GOODS AND GPS SYSTEM

Since these gps equipped and numbered compartments stop directly to their programmed stopping point, transfer of goods becomes more reliable, as it reduces the cases of theft and misplacement, the receiver can directly receive goods from the compartment on insertion of correct password set by the sender.
