homepage

The Lithium-ion battery will get fully charged in 1 hour.
Once it is fully charge it will run for 2.5 hours which can cover upto150 KM,6 trips of 23 KMs.
So in the duration of 4 hours the pod will get completely charged and makes 6 trips which equals36 trips per day.
The average cost of electric power in India is Rs.5 per KWH which makes the charging cost per pod per day Rs. 300.
since we will be charging the customer Rs.1/KM, the daily profit generated would be around Rs.1356.
Hence the total revenue generated by 1020 pods (As in case study will be 13.8 Lacs).
Permanent investments-
Pods-Rs.2 lac + 1 Lac(Maintenance).
A total of 30 Crores for 1020 pods.
Track- Construction @50 Lacs per kilometre.
Station- 50-70 Crores @35K/m2.
For Extra Construction-30 Crore.
Total- Rs.140 Crores.

Rate of Revenue generation----
13.8 Lacs per day = 150 Crore for 3 years.
Conclusion:-The rate that we have taken are well above the price estimations from various sources, still the system will recover its development cost in about 3 years. In contrast to the other available public transportation system, the recovery of the investment is fast enough for it to be considered as a viable option.
